# DataPulse Pricing Plans

## Overview

DataPulse offers three pricing tiers designed to scale with your data observability needs.

## Free Plan - $0/month

Perfect for individuals and small teams getting started with data observability.

### Features Included:
- ✅ Up to 5 data sources
- ✅ Up to 10 monitors
- ✅ Basic monitoring (freshness, volume, schema)
- ✅ 7 days of data retention
- ✅ Email notifications
- ✅ Community support
- ✅ Single user account

### Limitations:
- ❌ No API access
- ❌ No custom SQL monitors
- ❌ No Slack/PagerDuty integrations
- ❌ No SSO
- ❌ No data lineage

**Best for**: Personal projects, learning, proof of concept

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?
Yes, you can change plans at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your billing cycle.

### Do you offer annual billing?
Yes, save 20% with annual billing on Pro and Enterprise plans.

### Is there a free trial for Pro?
Yes, all new users get a 14-day free trial of Pro features.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, ACH transfers, and wire transfers (Enterprise only).

### What happens if I exceed my limits?
On the Free plan, you'll be prompted to upgrade. On Pro, overage charges apply for API requests. Enterprise has no hard limits.
